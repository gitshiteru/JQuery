History of Rome

There is archeological evidence of human occupation of the Rome area from at least 14,000 years, but the dense layer of much younger debris obscures Palaeolithic and Neolithic sites. Evindence of stone tools, pottery and stone weapons attest to at least 10,000 years of human presence.

Goverment of Rome

Rome constitures one of Italy's 8,101 communes, and is the largest both in terms of land area and population. It is governed by mayor, currently Gianni Alemanno, and a city council.
